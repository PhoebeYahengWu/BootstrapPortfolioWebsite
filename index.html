About Me

I’m Phoebe. If you are a Friends fan, this name should sound familiar.

I’m a graduate student at Baruch College studying information systems. I also passed the CPA and CISA exams while studying accounting at Baruch College, and I amassed experience in internal audit and IT maintenance at Deutsche Bank and Subotnick Financial Services Center. Upon completing my MS, I hope to work in the financial sector where I can apply my expertise in both accounting and technology.

Currently I intern at Jimmy Choo, a luxury shoes and accessories brand. I love that my team there invests in me and exposes me to many different tasks, from doing analysis on SAP software application to improving business processes related to financial reporting. I also worked as a front-end web development intern at Popshop, a platform that empowers users to book retail space. What I enjoyed most in that role was seeing my direct impact on our platform through the lines of codes I wrote.

Outside of work, I love Zumba! I was an aerobic dancing instructor for three years. I also love watching Stand-up comedy in my free time. Sometimes I try to throw some jokes into conversations with friends. Most times, they fall flat - but hey, I try.
